Overview

There is substantial interest in generating short, readable summaries of news articles. Serval companies providing short human-written summaries are top-rated among readers. But most existing companies only provide services in English or Hindi so far. While large scale datasets are available for a number of languages like English, Chinese, French, German, Spanish, Russian, and Turkish[1,2,3], no such datasets exist for any Indian languages [5]. With this shared task, we aim to bridge that gap for two major languages Hindi and Gujarati, having over 350 million and over 50 million speakers, respectively.
