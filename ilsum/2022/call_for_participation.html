Call for Participation

Task Description:
 
ILSUM provides a forum and a data challenge for research on text summarization. This year we offer this task in English, Hindi, Gujarati with 2 sub-tasks, on text summarization for news articles from various news websites for all three languages. Participants can choose to participate in one or both subtasks.
